2. ABOUT US PAGE
OUR STORY
The Beginning Before the Beginning
Two years as Marrian Foundation. A lifetime of giving.
Long before Marrian Foundation had a name, it had a heartbeat - the heartbeat of a young boy who learned that what matters most is not what you have, but what you choose to do with it.
He grew up in a family that struggled financially, studying at St. Joseph's, Coimbatore, with one clear goal in mind: work hard, get a good job, build a stable life. But one ordinary day, everything shifted. He found a ?500 note on the road, walked into a nearby old age home, and gave it away.
What he saw there changed him - elders the institution could barely accommodate, lives the world had quietly stopped noticing.
"In that moment, he understood that the purpose of living is giving."
Every weekend after that belonged to that home. He helped elders bathe and shave - simple acts of care given freely, with no money, no resources. Just love.
That seed grew into a small study centre at his own home in Palakkad, where village children came to study and grow. Several of them now work at some of the country's biggest companies.
In 2011, that same spirit grew further - into Dreams Alive: Be Human and Do or Die Trust, taking weekend visits to old age homes and orphanages across Coimbatore. It was built on a simple belief: that presence and dignity could change a life as much as money could.

A Name Changed. A Mission Continued.
Marrian Foundation was registered on our CEO's 39th birthday - 19th July 2024.
Not the start of something new, but the next chapter of a story already being written - in a child handed a scholarship, a grandmother given a day of joy at Marrian Farms, a rural youth handed a job and a purpose at a study centre.
The name changed. The mission never did.

A boy who chose to give.
A man who built a foundation on it.
The story was never really about the money. It was always about the heart.

VISION
To nurture a compassionate and inclusive society where every individual, regardless of background or circumstance, has access to equal opportunity, and the support needed to realise their full potential.
We envision communities strengthened by education, guided by ethical leadership, and uplifted through collective responsibility and care.

MISSION
To extend a helping hand with love, humaneness, and structured action by:
* Supporting the education and holistic development of children and youth from economically disadvantaged backgrounds.
* Building responsible and value-driven leaders for future generations.
* Providing care, protection, and assistance to vulnerable individuals and families.
* Strengthening institutions and communities through sustainable partnerships.
* Creating long-term impact through transparency, accountability, and compassionate service.

Our mission is not limited to offering aid; it is to build pathways of stability, self-reliance, and hope that empower individuals to thrive with confidence and dignity.
Our Approach
Marrian Foundation's work is guided by four principles:
* Monitoring - Close, day-to-day oversight of every active program.
* Strategic Planning - Every initiative is designed with clear, measurable outcomes.
* On-Ground Execution - From project planning to implementation, our team stays hands-on.
* Sustained Support - We stay engaged with the people we support well beyond the first intervention.

3. STUDY CENTRE PAGE
Reaching the Last Mile, One Village at a Time
Across Palakkad, Kerala, Marrian Foundation runs 13 active Study Centres spread over 11 villages, currently supporting 224 students and reaching close to 200 families.
For many children, the Study Centre is more than a place to study - it's a safe, structured space that fills the gap left by limited access to quality schooling. Our volunteers and coordinators work closely with families to keep attendance steady and address learning gaps early.
What Happens at a Study Centre
* Daily academic support and tuition
* Structured learning environment
* Close coordination with families
* Regular progress tracking

NURTURE SUPPORT PAGE
The Care That Doesn't Stop at Enrolments
A scholarship alone isn't enough to change a life - sustained support is. Every student under Nurture Scholarship is brought into Nurture Support, Marrian Foundation's ongoing care program.
What Nurture Support Provides
* Constant monitoring of each student's academic and personal progress
* Emergency assistance during family crises
* Career guidance and job opportunity support
* Counselling
* Special classes to reinforce learning
This is the layer that turns a one-time scholarship into a long-term relationship - one that follows the student from classroom to career.
